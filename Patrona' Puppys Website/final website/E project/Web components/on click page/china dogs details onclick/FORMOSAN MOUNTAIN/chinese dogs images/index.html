Description

The XIASI, a member of the Non-Sporting Group, is a small to medium-sized dog known for its friendly, outgoing, and adaptable nature. Standing at 14-17 inches and weighing 18-30 pounds, XIASI come in various colors, including white, black, gold, and brindle. With a life expectancy of 12-15 years, they are intelligent and eager to please, making positive reinforcement effective in training.
