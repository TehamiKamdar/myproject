Description

The Field Spaniel, a medium to large-sized dog in the Sporting Group, is known for its friendly and adaptable nature. Weighing 35–50 pounds and standing around 17–18 inches, they make affectionate family companions. Originally bred for flushing game, they excel in various activities, including dog sports. Regular exercise, grooming, and veterinary check-ups are essential for their well-being.
